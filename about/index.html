About iShare

iShare is a ministry of the Pacific Union Conference that is committed to empowering laity, youth and church leaders to live a lifestyle of evangelism – also known as "evange-living". iShare is both a lifestyle that is fragrant with the love of Jesus, passionate about soul winning, thankful for salvation by grace alone, and prayerful about Christ’s soon return, and a yearly conference that seeks to unite, not divide, challenge, not criticize, empower, not dictate, and love, not hate.

Our Mission
We seek to connect young people with Christ in a personal relationship that will bring spiritual revival and awaken the desire to do evangelism.
Our Vision
We provide training and resources for young people in the Pacific Union, fostering a year-round lifestyle of evangelism.
Our Beliefs

To love – God, His Son, and His Spirit as our Lord, Lawmaker, and Lover of our souls.

To uphold – The Bible, the testing point of all doctrines.

To appreciate – The Spirit of Prophecy, God's method of magnifying the Bible.

To support – and defend the Seventh-day Adventist Church as God's remant church.

To share – The Gospel of Grace and love to every nation, kindred, tongue, and people.
